BeePlus for Beekeeper

Features

Profile Hover Tooltip

Hover any avatar to see configurable profile fields.

📌

Sticky Pinned Chats

Keep important chats at the top of your list.

📊

Quick Polls

Create numbered-emoji polls from the composer.

Personal Stats

Track your own activity locally — never transmitted.

📥

Export Everything

Download own + team profiles as JSON or CSV.

🎨

Theme Tweaks

Compact, larger font, accessibility, custom CSS.
